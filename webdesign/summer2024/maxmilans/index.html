A picture of Nancy Harkness Love

"Nice young ladies don't do such things."

A concerned mother tried to give her daughter a piece of her mind, but to no avail. The stubborn daughter is Nancy Harkness Love, who was the leader of the WAFS, who later became the Executive in Air Transport Command of the WASPS, helped transport planes in World War II, and started military recognition for female pilots.
